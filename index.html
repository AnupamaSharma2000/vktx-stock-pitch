01 — INVESTMENT THESIS

Why the Market Is Wrong

VKTX has fallen ~67% from its 2024 peak of $99, not because the science failed — but because macro fears and competitive noise crowded out rational analysis. The Phase 3 VANQUISH trials just completed enrollment, and oral VK2735 Phase 3 is underway. This is maximum fear, minimum information — the ideal entry point.

📉

The Disconnect

Stock is priced as if VK2735 fails. But Phase 2 data showed 14.7% body weight reduction at 13 weeks for injectable, and 8.2% at 13 weeks for oral — competitive with Lilly's Zepbound.

💊

Dual-Formulation Edge

VK2735 has both injectable AND oral forms in late-stage trials. Most GLP-1 competitors are single-formulation. This doubles Viking's addressable patient population.

🎯

M&A Optionality

Pfizer, AstraZeneca, and Roche all lack a market-ready GLP-1 therapy. A successful Phase 3 readout makes Viking an obvious acquisition target at a significant premium.

🏦

Well Capitalized

$705M in cash and short-term investments, virtually zero debt. At current burn rate (~$279M/year), VKTX has 2.5+ years of runway — through all critical Phase 3 readouts.
